### **Al-Amri Engineering Consultancy**  
#### **Projects Portfolio**

### **1. Al-Sabah College for Science and Technology in Socotra Governorate**  

**Location and Importance:**  
The Al-Sabah College for Science and Technology project aims to establish an educational institution specializing in science and technology on the island of Socotra, one of the world's most important natural reserves. The college will 

provide an advanced educational environment to support scientific research and innovation, contributing to the development of higher education and enhancing the skills of local professionals in scientific and technical fields.  

---

### **2. Al-Ittihad Complex in the Temporary Capital, Aden**  

**Location and Importance:**  
The Al-Ittihad Complex in the temporary capital, Aden, is a strategic project aimed at supporting infrastructure and 

providing integrated facilities for housing and work. The complex includes residential units and administrative offices equipped with the latest technologies, making it a vital center for business development and improving the quality of life in the city.  

---

### **3. Al-Ahlam Chalets Complex in Khor Maksar**  

**Location and Importance:**  
The Al-Ahlam Chalets Complex in Khor Maksar is a luxurious tourist project featuring a collection of 

modern and comfortable chalets. The project aims to attract tourists and provide a unique accommodation experience in one of the most vibrant areas of Aden, with stunning views of the sea and picturesque nature.  

---

### **4. Al-Sahel Al-Arabi Resort in Al-Ilm, Abyan**  

**Location and Importance:**  
The Al-Sahel Al-Arabi Resort in Al-Ilm, Abyan, is a unique tourist destination that combines breathtaking nature with exceptional 

hotel services. The resort offers a luxurious experience for visitors with integrated recreational facilities, making it an ideal destination for families and individuals seeking relaxation and entertainment.  

---

### **5. Rehabilitation of Al-Shaheed Salmin Park in Abyan Governorate**  

**Location and Importance:**  
The park is located in Zinjibar City, the center of Abyan Governorate, known for its cultural heritage and 

natural diversity between fertile plains and coastal areas. The park is one of the oldest public parks in the governorate and has been a symbol of family recreation and community gatherings for years. However, it has deteriorated due to neglect and damage caused by security conditions.  

**Project Objectives:**  
1. Reviving community memory by restoring the park as a vital space for social interaction.  
2. Improving the quality of life by providing safe and healthy green spaces.  
3. Enhancing local tourism by 

making the park an attractive destination for families and visitors.  

**Project Components:**  
- Development of infrastructure, including pedestrian pathways with solar-powered lighting.  
- Recreational areas with safe playgrounds for children and an open-air theater for cultural events.  
- Environmental touch with the planting of local trees and the creation of a small artificial lake using recycled rainwater.  

**Expected Impact:**  
- Socially: Strengthening community 

ties and providing inclusive spaces for all age groups.  
- Economically: Creating job opportunities and boosting local businesses.  
- Environmentally: Increasing green spaces and improving air quality.  

---

### **6. Headquarters Building of the National Company for Humanitarian Transfers ‚Äì Khor Maksar**  

**Location and Importance:**  
The building is located in Khor Maksar, Aden, near Al-Khair 

Mosque, in a central area close to the airport, enhancing its accessibility. The project serves as the headquarters of a national company operating in the humanitarian transfers sector, a vital sector in Yemen due to the significant role of external aid and remittances in the local economy.  

**Project Components:**  
- 6 floors with a modern design reflecting the company‚Äôs identity.  
- Administrative offices, a conference hall, a spacious reception area, and integrated service facilities.  
- Multi-level parking and advanced 

security systems.  

**Architectural Design and Features:**  
- Modern style with glass facades for natural lighting.  
- Advanced security and privacy systems.  
- Sustainable features such as thermal insulation and energy-efficient lighting.  

**Economic and Social Impact:**  
- Improved work environment for employees.  
- Enhanced humanitarian operations.  
- Increased confidence of local and 

international partners.
